Risk Scoring Methodology

The AI Risk Prediction Engine evaluates counsellors' probability of missing their month-end targets before the cycle closes.

Evaluation Factors:

  • Target achievement progress (35% weight): Pro-rata admissions gap versus month-end projection.
  • Effective Call Ratio (20% weight): Percent of connected calls extending past pitch threshold.
  • Productivity effort (15% weight): Average daily dial volumes.
  • Talk Time Engagement (15% weight): Focus and depth of conversations.
  • Attendance (15% weight): Consistent day-to-day dialer availability.
Green (Safe) < 30
Yellow (Warning) 30 - 60
Red (High Risk) > 60

Lead Pipeline Quality Breakdown

Leads are classified automatically based on user interaction columns in Excel:
  • 🔥 Hot Leads: Admissions EMI started & Full Payment On Spot. High closing potential.
  • ⚡ Warm Leads: Admissions forms filled or exploratory interest flags.
  • ❄️ Cold Leads: Explored only. Reached connected status but no progression.

Fair Performance Scoring (Expected vs Actual)

Evaluating counsellors solely on overall conversion rates is unfair if leads are distributed unequally. This model calculates an **Expected Conversion Rate** adjusted for lead quality, then tags the counselor as Overperforming, Average, or Underperforming.
